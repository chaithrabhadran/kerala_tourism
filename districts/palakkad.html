History



History of Palakkad In earlier times Palakkad was also known as Palakkattuchery. The word “Palakkad” from the word Palai nilam, which means “dry lands”. The name has two fusion of Malayalam Words Pala means a tree Alstonia scholar is that is found abundantly in Palakkad and second one is Kadu which means forest.
